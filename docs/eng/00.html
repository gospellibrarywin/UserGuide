Introduction

Welcome to Gospel Library for Windows.

Gospel Library is an official application of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Gospel Library for Windows gives you access to thousands of pages of inspired gospel content to enrich your daily gospel study and to strengthen your relationship with Heavenly Father.

Study Often

"... [F]east upon the words of Christ; for behold, the words of Christ will tell you all things what ye should do."

2 Nephi 32:3

In Gospel Library, you can read and mark your scriptures, listen to the words of prophets and apostles, view videos like Bible Videos and Mormon Messages, and more. This guide will give you a general overview of the features offered in Gospel Library for Windows.
